Chocolate Chip Cookies

My Favorite Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe!!

Chocolate chip cookies are a timeless classic. Great any time during the year but especially during the Holiday season!

Ingredients

  • 2-1/2 cups flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 (12 oz.) bag chocolate chips
  • 1 cup coarsley chopped nuts (optional)
  • ...and all your love!

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 350 degrees. Line cookie sheet with parchment paper and set aside. On another sheet of parchment paper, combine flour, baking soda, salt, and cinnamon; set aside.
  2. Beat together butter, brown sugar and sugar in a large mixing bowl, with an electric mixer until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  3. Add flour mixture to butter mixture; beat until well blended. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts. Drop by rounded tablespoons onto parchment-lined cookie sheet
  4. Bake 10 to 12 minutes or until lightly browned and remove from oven. Let cookies cool for 1 minutes and enjoy!
  5. TURN OFF OVEN YA DUMMY!!
