Banana Bread

My Favorite Banana Bread Recipe!!

This is a great recipe for when you banana's are too ripe. I mean you can't just throw those things out!

Ingredients

  • 2-3 ripe bananas
  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 3/4 cups sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 egg beaten
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • a pinch of salt
  • ...and all your love!

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a mixing bowl, mash the ripe bananas with a fork until completely smooth. Stir the melted butter into the mashed bananas.
  3. Mix in baking soda and salt. Stir in sugar, beaten egg, and vanilla extract. Mix in the flour.
  4. Pour the batter you just made into a 4 in. by 8 in. pan. Place pan in oven for 50 minutes.
  5. Check occasionally until golden brown and then remove from oven. Enjoy!
  6. TURN OFF OVEN YA DUMMY!!
